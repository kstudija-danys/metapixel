What it does?

Using AI algorithms, detects faces, objects and scenes in your photos.

Metapixel.APP automatically writes the contextual keywords into your picture metadata.

Voila! Your pictures are now searchable by contextual keywords.

Works in 3 easy steps

Step 1: Select tags

Select the folder you want to automatically tag. Select the type of tags you want to be added to the pictures.

The app detects people, objects (e.g. building, forest, beach) and scenes (eg. sunset, afternoon, clouds) with new custom tagging libraries available as an option.

Step 2: Tagging

The app does all the tagging work for you.

Please note, that the more pictures and the more things you want to tag, the more time it will take for the process to complete. We recommend an Intel® or AMD processor with 64-bit support 2 GHz or faster processor with at least 4GB of RAM and a dedicated GPU.

Step 3: Review

Tagging complete!

You may review tags picture by picture and add your own tags. Every tag is assigned a degree of confidence. After sampling a few pictures, you may accept tags in bulk for all the pictures based on the confidence level.

Step 4: Face recognition

These faces were detected in your library. Who are these people? Please, name them. The name tag will be automatically added to all the pictures where this person is present.

Why Metapixel.APP?

AI powered

Metapixel.APP uses AI algorithms to detect faces and objects to automatically tag your pictures - no manual input required.

On device

Metapixel.APP is a downloadable Windows software. It works on your device - no cloud uploads or internet connection required.

Fits your workflow

Metapixel.APP does not take over your traditional picture management workflow. The keywords are stored as EXIF metadata.
